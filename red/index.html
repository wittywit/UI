Manifesto

We hold the document still.
The world moves through it.

There is no homepage.
There is no scroll.
There is only the object—
held, examined, released.

Every surface is a different
reading of the same truth:
form follows intention,
intention follows silence.

The hands that hold this document
are not made of flesh.
They are made of attention.
And attention is the only material
that has ever mattered.

This is INEX.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

INEX is an editorial and design studio exploring the intersection of speculative technology, material culture, and the document as object. We treat every project as a living artefact—subject to revision, resistant to resolution, insisting on being examined.

This interface is itself the work. The hands do not move. The content does. The form is the message.

APPROACH

Form follows intention. Intention follows silence. We work slowly, at museum pace, in the belief that the most durable design is the kind that refuses to explain itself.
